DDMM_Suite

Data-Driven Decision Making for Management: Statistics for Managers

Live

A Quarto-based statistics textbook for management students and practitioners, covering descriptive statistics, probability, hypothesis testing, correlation, regression, and non-parametric methods. The suite produces six output formats from a single source repository: an HTML book, RevealJS lecture decks, worked examples, instructor notes, Moodle XML quiz banks, and YouTube outline scripts. The rendered materials are published online and the project is maintained in a version-controlled GitHub repository.
