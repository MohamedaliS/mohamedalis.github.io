Mohammed Ali Sharafuddin

Senior Lecturer, Villa College — Malé, Maldives PhD Candidate, PIMSAT Tamil Nadu Marketing & Statistics

Management academic with 25 years of experience in teaching, research, mentoring, administration, and consulting. He teaches management, marketing, tourism management, market research, and services marketing, with a strong emphasis on market research methods, digital marketing, and marketing analytics.

His research focuses on the sustainability, resilience, and digitalisation of service sectors, using data-driven approaches to enhance service quality and operational performance, published across more than 23 Scopus-indexed journal articles in venues including Sustainability, Global Business Review, Administrative Sciences, and Encyclopedia.
